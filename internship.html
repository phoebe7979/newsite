UX research internship at Essential.
In summer 2019, I interned at Essential's UX team as the only researcher. The exprience working at a fast-pace consumer electronics startup has been challenging yet rewarding!
*This is an NDA-protected project.

Internship duration
11 weeks
June - August 2019
Main delievereables
A UX strategy report for a new product category
Three usability study reports
Two comparative analyses
Methodologies
Literature review
Comparative analysis
Workshop
Use cases and scenarios
Usablity testing
WHAT I DID

Generative and evaluative research

Working as the only UX reesarcher within a team that developes at full-speed, I was able to work on research studies on different stages of the design process.

On one hand, I worked closely with my supervisors on identifying potential user groups and usage scenarios by means of literature review, workshop, and interviews. On the other hand, I conducted regular usability studies for new features and provided data-informed suggestions for the designers.

Having the opportunity to touch on both generative and evaluative resarch projects has allowed me to view the design process holistically.
